  • Final Project

    About: The idea was to see whether you can influence by dancing and bobing your head to your (imaginary) beat. Unfortunately the IMU wasn't working as well as I anticipated. It's hard figuring out where the player is looking, whether it's the top left, bottom right, or so forth. It's an interesting proof of concept though, which could definitely use better music in further development.

    How to use: Please use an Android phone, in a VR headset (Cardboard or somethign of the sort). Currently, the imu number at which point things are left or right of is 180. You may need to turn around in order to get to 180. This is the "Direction" number displayed on the screen. The two values after it are which way you are looking, upper left, upper right, bottom left, bottom right. It calculates the sum of where you look over over a 55 frames to determine where you're looking. Enjoy!
